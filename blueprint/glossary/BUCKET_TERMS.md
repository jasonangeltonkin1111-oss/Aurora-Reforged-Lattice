# Bucket Terms

Major bucket means broad class.

Minor bucket means subgroup inside broad class.

Examples:

- `FOREX` -> `FOREX_EXOTICS`
- `CRYPTO` -> `CRYPTO_ALTCOINS`
- `METALS` -> `METALS_GOLD_SILVER`
- `INDICES` -> `INDICES_US`
- `STOCKS` -> `STOCKS_TECH`
