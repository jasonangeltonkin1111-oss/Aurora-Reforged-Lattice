### Closure-blocker language

A later roadmap item may be **source-landed** while closure remains blocked. A later roadmap item may be **runtime-visible** while calibration/validation remains blocked. No later scoring/ranking/deep-support closure claim is valid while B2, B3, or B4 remains materially unresolved unless the later pass explicitly names the unresolved prerequisite as a proof-hold and avoids closure-ready wording.

### Source-landed vs runtime-proven vs closure-ready

- **Source-landed** means the intended code/control wording exists in the current source pack.
- **Runtime-proven** means fresh MT5 runtime artifacts/logs/screenshots agree that the behavior works in the live path.
- **Closure-ready** means the required prerequisite proof classes are satisfied or explicitly held, and no unresolved blocker is being hidden by later progress wording.
