# Deep Path Non-Regression and Publication Guards

## Why this lane exists

The deep path is now one of the healthier parts of the system.
That makes it more dangerous to touch casually.
This lane exists to protect it while ordinary dossier and throughput work happens elsewhere.

## Protection targets

- selected-symbol deep root preservation
- Current Focus same-symbol deep support preservation
- Global Top 10 L0–6 child family completion
- same-symbol non-downgrade
- publication identity continuity

## What this lane must do

1. Make deep non-regression rules explicit in code and office planning.
2. Guard deep outputs from weaker ordinary or baseline publication paths.
3. Preserve lawful split-role behavior between board and support artifacts.
4. Preserve deep child verification and publication identity rules.

## What this lane must not do

- do not redesign deep logic from zero
- do not merge deep ownership into ordinary dossier ownership
- do not let ordinary “cleanup” touch deep payload semantics without proof
- do not change Global Top 10 identity logic unless a fresh regression is proven

## Likely primary code owners

- `mt5/runtime/ASC_Dispatcher.mqh`
- `mt5/core/ASC_RuntimeState.mqh`
- `mt5/artifacts/ASC_DossierWriter.mqh`
- `mt5/artifacts/ASC_CurrentFocusWriter.mqh`

- `mt5/deep_selective_analysis/ASC_DeepSelectiveAnalysisEngine.mqh`
- any directly called deep-family verification helper that truly owns publication identity or non-downgrade behavior

## Required regression fences

- no deep root overwrite by ordinary dossier path
- no child-family identity drift in Global Top 10
- no current-focus support downgrade after lawful deep completion
- no owner drift from board toward dossier/current-focus

## Success conditions

- a deep-selected symbol stays deep after ordinary refresh pressure
- Global Top 10 child family stays L0–6 complete where triggered
- board remains primary authority while support artifacts remain support-only

## Failure conditions

- any deep artifact drops back to ordinary or shell posture
- same-symbol non-downgrade is weakened
- board authority becomes ambiguous
