# ASC Summary and Publication Contract

## Purpose

Summaries are late, selective, and secondary.
They must not outrun symbol-file truth.

## Summary emphasis

Summaries should prefer:
- runtime posture
- Layer 1 counts and readiness
- shortlist/top-set posture
- promoted deep-set posture

Summaries should avoid:
- broad full-universe dumps
- writer-side ranking math
- render-time recomputation

## Promotion-aware summary law

Once Layer 4 and Layer 5 are active, summary surfaces should foreground the promoted operator set first rather than raw universe density.
