# Phase 06 — Market Board

## Purpose

Create the operator board from committed L3/L4 snapshots.

## Must include

- snapshot IDs
- account/exposure summary
- system health
- Global Top 10
- Top 5 per major bucket
- Top 5 per minor bucket
- best 3/5/10 upload suggestions
- excluded candidates and numeric reasons
- retry/schedule status
- trade_permission=false

## Must avoid

- proof wall
- unavailable spam
- route diagnostics unless contradiction exists
- HUD
