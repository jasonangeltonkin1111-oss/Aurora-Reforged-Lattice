# Known ASC Failures To Prevent

## Failures seen or implied from uploaded ASC outputs

- Board/dossier/SDP snapshot mismatch.
- Global Top 10 child sets and order not matching board.
- L3/L4 owner truth not transport-bound into symbol outputs.
- Too much diagnostic prose in operator files.
- Too many unavailable fields making useful sections look degraded.
- File presence mistaken for currentness.
- Giant dispatcher pressure.
- HUD complexity too early.
- Route helpers and family routes becoming hard to reason about.
- Placeholder/unknown state not clearly separated from ready truth.
- Trade permission false repeated everywhere instead of clean boundary sections.

## ARL prevention

- L3 and L4 committed snapshots.
- Board consumes only committed L4.
- Dossier references board/L4/L3 snapshot IDs.
- Atomic temp/readback/promote.
- Numeric-first output.
- Required/optional/irrelevant missing field classes.
- No HUD until core proof.
- No new route during repair.
